Malware Detection Circuit

DE 12/04/20

Millions of people are affected by different types of malware each year, placing their information at risk. It can be confusing for people to identify which types of malware exists on their devices while knowing what to do about it. For this project, my team and I designed, simulated, and breadboarded a circuit that will identify the types of malware present on a device based on certain characteristics. With these outputs, an appropriate method of eliminating the malware will also be outputted. I was responsible for creating a portion of the circuit which dealt with a specific type of malware called the "worm." I made a circuit using a circuit design software called Multisim. After making it on multisim I made the circuit on a breadboard. For further information please refer to our documentation here.
